# The future of work

> ielts academic 16

- But these enhancements are not without consequences: 但是這些增強並非沒有後果

## Phrases

- the advent of: 在...出現之前
  - synonym: the arrival of
- the extent to: 在...程度上
  - synonym: the degree to
- be deterred from: 被阻止
  - synonym: be prevented from
